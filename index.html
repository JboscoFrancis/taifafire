FIRE SAFETY

  • store flammable and combustible materials in secure, fire resistant storage areas
  • ensure no ignition sources are close to flammable and combustible storage
  • remove unneccessary heat sources and all naked flames from the workplace
  • switch off and unplug electrical devices when not in use
  • enforce no smoking policy in all enclosed buildings
  • reduce the potential for oxygen supply by shutting all windows, doors and any non-essential ventilation systems
  • Carry out a fire risk assessment regularly
